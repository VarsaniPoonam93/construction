Buddy Story

Buddy is a wonderful and witty 19 year old young man. He loves Church, Chuck E. Cheese videos, and Chicken Nuggets; in no particular order.

Each morning during the school year, Buddy waits for the bus at the end of the driveway. Even Though his older sister, Lucy, could drive him to school a half an hour later! But it’s the routine and The accomplishment he gets from riding the bus by himself to school. Right before he boards the bus, He says, “have a good day” to me or my wife, Pamela. As parents, we couldn’t be more proud of him As we are constantly reminded of his desire to wish others goodwill.

His awareness of his community, and all of the community helpers as he says, is ever present.

Fragile X Syndrome can cause, among other disabilities, epilepsy in some individuals. Since Buddy was 5 years old, he has needed the help of our local police and EMS providers to ensure he is safely Moved to the hospital. It seems fitting that he is always wishing them well, and, from time to time, Dressing up as them for Halloween.
